About Us

Built for people who want results, not theory

OxTech Academy started because most AI education out there is either too academic or too shallow — long on theory, short on anything you can actually use. We built a different kind of classroom: one where every lesson ends with something you shipped, not just something you understood.

We teach the way skills are actually used in the field — building AI agents, writing prompts that produce real output, and applying digital tools to real business problems. No filler modules, no recycled slideshows.

Every course is taught live and iterated on based on what's actually working in the industry right now, not what worked two years ago.
