FAQ

Questions people ask before enrolling

Do I need any coding experience to join?

No. The "AI for Everyday Life" and "Practical Digital Skills" tracks are built for complete beginners. The "AI Development & Agents" track is more technical, but we start from fundamentals and build up from there.

Are classes live or pre-recorded?

All courses are taught live, so you can ask questions in real time and get feedback on what you're building. Sessions are recorded so you can revisit them, but the core teaching happens live.

How long does each course take?

Most tracks run a few weeks, with live sessions a couple of times a week plus hands-on project work in between. Exact schedules are shared during enrollment based on the track you choose.

What do I actually walk away with?

A shipped project relevant to your track — a working AI agent, a portfolio of prompts, or a digital skill set you can put on a CV or use for freelance work — not just certificates or slides.

Do you offer payment plans or refunds?

Reach out to us via the contact form below and we'll walk you through the current payment options and refund policy for your track.
